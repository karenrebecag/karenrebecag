## 🧩 How I work

I think of myself as the person who makes design systems real. I'll sit with designers in Figma, extract the tokens, build the component library, and ship it to production — then come back and make the animation feel right.

I'm drawn to products that care about **craft**. Not just "does it work" but "does it feel right". That attention to detail is what makes the difference between a good product and one people actually enjoy using.
